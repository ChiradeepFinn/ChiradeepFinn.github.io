About Me

I am currently a high school student with a growing passion for technology as it relates to both human expression and human connection. As my interest in computers grew so did my interests in AI, Web Development and Creative Digital Production. I also have an appreciation for music and sound design. I incorporate digital tools into creating emotionally rich and expressive soundscapes. I love combining Technology and Creativity to see how software can help further artistic ideas and interactive experiences. I am also very interested in Psychology and Communication particularly, how people perceive things, interact with each other and form their own communities. Ultimately, my long term goals are to develop projects that will integrate both technology, and human intuition to promote both innovation and community building.

My goal is to build creative projects that combine technology with digital creativity while building a global community for collaboration and innovation.
